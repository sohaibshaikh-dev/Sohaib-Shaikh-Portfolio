# about

About me

I am a Creative Director and UI/UX Designer based in Karachi, Pakistan, working across web development and print media. I enjoy transforming complex ideas and challenges into simple, modern, and user-friendly designs.

My focus is on creating websites and visual experiences that are not only visually appealing but also functional, intuitive, and easy to use. I bring a personal and thoughtful approach to every project, ensuring each product feels unique, engaging, and effective.

My goal is to clearly communicate a client’s message and brand identity through creative and professional design solutions. I have successfully worked on a variety of web and design projects for different clients and brands.
